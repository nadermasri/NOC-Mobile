## ASO (App Store Optimization) Notes

- Lead with "network diagnostics" and "monitoring" — these are the highest-volume search terms
- "NOC" itself has low search volume but high intent — keep it in the name for the right audience
- Avoid generic competitor names ("ping app", "speedtest") that are dominated by giants
- The phrase "infrastructure monitoring" + "alerts" targets DevOps engineers searching for solutions
