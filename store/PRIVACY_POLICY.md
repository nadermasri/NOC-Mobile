# Privacy Policy for Pocket NOC

**Last updated:** May 26, 2026
**Effective date:** May 26, 2026

Pocket NOC ("we", "our", or "the app") is a network diagnostics and infrastructure monitoring application. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Pocket NOC.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account (optional), we collect:
- **Email address** — used for authentication and account recovery
- **Display name** (optional) — shown in your profile
- **Password** — stored as a bcrypt hash; we never see or store your plain-text password

### 1.2 Information You Generate by Using the App

When you use Pocket NOC's diagnostic and monitoring features, the app stores:
- **Targets you save** (hostnames, IP addresses, ports)
- **Diagnostic results** (ping latency, DNS records, port status, TLS certificate info, HTTP responses, traceroute hops)
- **Monitor configurations** (what you're monitoring and how often)
- **Alert history** (notifications generated by your monitors)

All of this data is stored **locally on your device** by default. If you sign in to an account, this data is also synced to our servers so you can access it from multiple devices.

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do **not** collect:
- Your location
- Your contacts
- Your photos, files, or other device data
- Advertising identifiers
- Analytics or tracking data
- Any personal information beyond what's listed in section 1.1

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:
- Authenticate you when you sign in
- Sync your diagnostic data across your devices
- Provide AI-powered explanations of your diagnostic results (the diagnostic data is sent to our server, which processes it locally — it is not sent to any third-party AI service)
- Send you alerts based on monitors you've configured

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing, advertising, or any other purpose.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- Data on your device is stored in an encrypted local database
- Data on our servers is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted by Railway (railway.com) in the European Union
- All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS (TLS 1.2 or higher)
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt; we cannot recover your password if you lose it

## 4. Network Diagnostic Targets

Pocket NOC performs network operations (ping, DNS lookup, port checks, etc.) against hostnames and IP addresses **that you specify**. These operations are sent directly from your device to the target you entered. We do not monitor, log, or restrict which targets you check.

You are responsible for ensuring you have permission to perform diagnostic checks against any third-party targets. Pocket NOC is intended for diagnosing networks and infrastructure you own or are authorized to test.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

- **Local data**: deleted when you uninstall the app or use the in-app "Clear All Data" option
- **Cloud data**: retained while your account is active
- **Account deletion**: you can permanently delete your account and all associated data from within the app at any time (Settings → Account → Delete Account)

When you delete your account, all your data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

## 6. Third-Party Services

Pocket NOC uses the following third-party services:
- **Railway** (railway.com) — hosts our backend API and database (EU region)
- **GitHub** (github.com) — hosts our open-source code

We do not embed any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or social-media SDKs in the app.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Pocket NOC is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have, please contact us and we will delete it.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
- Access the data we hold about you
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
- Export your data in a portable format
- Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

## 9. International Data Transfers

If you use Pocket NOC from outside the European Union, please be aware that your data will be transferred to and stored on servers in the EU.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via the app. Continued use of Pocket NOC after a change means you accept the updated policy.
