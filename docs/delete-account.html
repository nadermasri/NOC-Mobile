Delete Your Account

Remove your NOC Mobile account and all associated data

You can permanently delete your NOC Mobile account and all associated data directly from within the app, or by contacting us by email.

Option 1 — Delete from the app (instant)

1
Open NOC Mobile on your device
2
Tap the Settings tab (gear icon)
3
Tap Account
4
Scroll down and tap Delete Account
5
Confirm when prompted — your account and all data will be deleted immediately

Option 2 — Request by email

If you no longer have access to the app, email us and we will delete your account manually within 7 days:

Email: naderbilalmasri@gmail.com
Subject: Delete my NOC Mobile account
Include: The email address associated with your account

What gets deleted: your account credentials, all saved targets, diagnostic history, monitor configurations, and alert history. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Data deleted

  • Email address and password hash
  • Display name (if set)
  • Saved diagnostic targets
  • Diagnostic results and history
  • Monitor configurations
  • Alert history

All server-side data is permanently removed within 30 days of account deletion.
